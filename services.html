A counselling service designed to help you understand and learn from life events

I will work with you to:

  • Bring your experience to a secure, accepting and caring environment.
  • Offer you safe place to be with whatever emotions you are feeling.
  • To support you to understand what ever feeling arises- there are no right or wrong feelings.
  • To explore how your unique experience of what is happening is affecting you in each moment
  • Find a language and dialogue to support expression of your experience and needs.
  • Gain insight
  • Explore coping strategies
  • Navigate the changes that you are living with
  • Recognise mechanisms that can support your individual process
  • Find ways of adaptation and resilience
  • Anchors to bring you peace stability and order
